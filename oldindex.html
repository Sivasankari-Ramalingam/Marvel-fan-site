MARVEL'S ORIGIN

Marvel Comics, Inc. (formerly known as Marvel Comics and Entertainment Group, Inc.) is an American multinational publisher and film studio based in New York City. It is one of the world's largest and most successful publishers of comic books, graphic novels, and video games. 1961-1963 was Marvel's "Silver Age", marking a transition out of Timely Comics, into the Marvel Comics name we know today. Readers were introduced to creators Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, Steve Ditko, and the original Bullpen, with stories that embraced a modern brand of hero. This is where it all began!
